About

5 Guys Brass & Ties is a brass quintet comprised of professional musicians. The group performs in the Central Pennsylvania region for weddings, church functions, parties, community events and other special occasions. They are comfortable performing any genre of music and are well versed in many styles.

The Musicians

Jordan Canner, Trumpet

Jordan is the Director of Bands at Huntingdon Area High School in Huntingdon, PA. He earned his undergraduate degree in Music Education from Indiana University of Pennsylvania. Jordan performs in several local ensembles in the area as well.

Rick Kane, Trumpet

Rick is the Director of Bands at Mount Union Area High School and an Adjunct Professor at the Pennsylvania Highlands Community College. He earned his undergraduate degree in Music Education from Indiana University of Pennsylvania and his masters of conducting from Messiah in 2015. Rick also performs in local community ensembles.

Tim Sarvey, Trombone

Tim is pursuing the field of web development, but has earned an undergraduate degree in Music Education from Indiana University of Pennsylvania and a masters degree in Music Technology from Indiana University Purdue University of Indianapolis. Tim performs locally throughout the year and is member of the Big Band Sound out of Mount Union, PA.

Steve Rodgers, Euphonium/Altonium

Steve is the Director of Bands at the Bedford Area High School in Bedford, PA. He earned his undergraduate degree in Music Education from Slippery Rock University and his masters in Music Education from Kent State University. Along with his duties in “5 Guys”, Steve is the principal euphonium for the Musicians’ Concert Band in Butler, Pennsylvania. Steve also occasionally performs on trumpet with the Bedford-based rock band “FBI”, as a member of the Brass Band Trio “The Vin #’s”.

Larry Sarvey, Tuba

Larry is the Director of Bands at the Everett Area Middle/High School in Everett, PA. He earned his undergraduate degree in Music Education from Indiana University of Pennsylvania. Larry performs throughout the year in various ensembles.
